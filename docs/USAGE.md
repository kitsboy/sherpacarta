## Quick Start (2 minutes)

1. **Visit** [sherpacarta.org](https://sherpacarta.org)
2. **Read** the hero and mission — privacy as a human right
3. **Open the charter** — big green button, or press **C**
4. **Sign** — scroll to *Sign the Charter* or press **G**
5. **Share** — official channels only: [@give_bit](https://twitter.com/give_bit), Nostr `kimi@giveabit.io`, [GitHub](https://github.com/kitsboy/sherpacarta)

No account. No cookies. Everything stays in your browser unless you choose Nostr publish.

## Signing the Charter

1. Scroll to **Sign the Charter** (or **G**)
2. Enter your name (real, pseudonym, or anonymous)
3. Pick your country
4. Click **Sign**
5. Optional: **Connect Nostr** to publish your signature to relays

**Quick start checklist** on the sign page tracks: charter opened, signed, link copied, Nostr copied, calculator run.

---

## Reading the Charter (114 Articles)

- **Open modal** — hero button, **C**, or ⌘K → *Open Full Charter*
- **Table of contents** — sidebar inside modal
- **Search** — ⌘K → *Search Charter*, or browser ⌘F inside modal
- **Star articles** — stars appear on sign page for quick return
- **Resume reading** — *Resume Art. X* button on articles section
- **Read aloud** — charter modal → Read Aloud (text-to-speech)
- **Download** — ⌘K → *Download Charter* (.txt)
- **Print** — ⌘K → *Print Charter*

Essential articles bar: **Art. 11** (Privacy), **Art. 12** (Data Sovereignty), **Art. 13** (Surveillance Capitalism), **Art. 61** (Algorithmic Rights).

## Nostr (Optional)

1. Install extension: **Alby**, **nos2x**, or **Primal**
2. Sign section → **Nostr setup** wizard (or ⌘K → *Nostr Setup Wizard*)
3. **Connect Nostr** — pubkey stays local; keys never touch our servers
4. Propose **amendments** — published to relays when connected
5. Copy **NIP-05**: `kimi@giveabit.io` (footer or ⌘K)
6. QR for NIP-05: ⌘K → *QR — Nostr NIP-05*
