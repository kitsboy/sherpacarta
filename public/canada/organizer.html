Log a paper batch

After a clipboards event, log how many wet signatures you collected. We store count + province + optional event label only — not the names from the sheets (you keep the paper).

Still present the physical sheets via an MP for Parliamentary effect. This log only strengthens the public campaign counter.

Clipboard script (30 seconds)

“Hi — we’re collecting a federal petition to modernize digital privacy rights in Canada. One sheet for the whole country: if you’re in BC, Ontario, anywhere, you just write your city and province. No ID needed, any age. Ink here counts for Parliament when an MP presents it; you can also scan the QR to join the online campaign.”
