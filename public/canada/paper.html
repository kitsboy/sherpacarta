Print petition sheets

Civic-grade letter sheets: plain-language summary, formal prayer (legal), roomy signature lines, and a QR so people can also join the digital campaign. Ink on this page is for Parliament / the Legislature. The QR is campaign + education — not a substitute for a wet signature.

Open QR landing Digital campaign sign
How to use in the field
  • Print single-sided, black & white, actual size (100% — not “fit to page”).
  • Clipboard + blue/black pen. Original ink only — no photocopied signatures.
  • Federal: aim for 25+ valid signatures before asking an MP to present.
  • Every sheet must keep the full prayer text (don’t cut the top off).
  • Invite scanners: “Sign in ink here — or scan to support online too.”

PETITION TO THE HOUSE OF COMMONS
IN PARLIAMENT ASSEMBLED

“Privacy is a birthright — not a product feature.”

Why this matters

  • Your data and private life should not be a product sold by default.
  • When algorithms score jobs, credit, or speech, you deserve a plain-language explanation.
  • Federal privacy law must catch up to AI-era power — rights online should only expand.
