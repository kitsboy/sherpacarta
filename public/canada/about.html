What we are

SherpaCarta is the Global Digital Magna Carta — 114 articles protecting digital privacy, data sovereignty, freedom of expression, and algorithmic rights for every person on Earth. We begin in Canada, with British Columbia as our first provincial beachhead.

How signing works (dual-track)

Track A — Campaign: Name/pseudonym, province, citizen/resident attestation. Optional passkey, Nostr, or device key. SHA-256 receipt. Optional privacy-first campaign total (hash + province + method).

Track B — Paper: Printable federal/BC sheets with wet signatures an MP/MLA can present.

Track B — Official e-petition: Only on ourcommons.ca after MP authorization (e-###). Email confirm; no ID; no minimum age; 500 signatures.

Referendum & polling vision

We are building a privacy-preserving referendum mechanism: SHA-256 ballot hashes, Nostr uniqueness, passkeys, and OpenTimestamps anchors via Satohash. Template ready for satohash.io/templates. EU eIDAS expansion planned.
