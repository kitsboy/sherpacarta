90-minute agenda

0:00 — Why a Digital Magna Carta? (10 min)

0:10 — Read Art. 11 aloud (5 min)

0:15 — BC legislative path — model bill overview (15 min)

0:30 — Breakout: one MLA action per attendee (20 min)

0:50 — Live sign at sherpacarta.org/#sign (10 min)

1:00 — Q&A + coalition signup (20 min)

1:20 — Close: #CanadaBCChallenge (10 min)

Materials needed

  • Projector + sherpacarta.org
  • Printed MP executive briefing (2 pages)
  • Printed federal petition sheets
  • QR: canada/join or paper sheet QR
  • Optional: model bill handouts

Facilitator tips

  • Stamp charter on Satohash before the event
  • Invite local privacy NGO representative
  • Collect photos only with consent — practice what we preach
