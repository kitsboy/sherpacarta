Municipal Safe Harbour Kit

← BC Challenge

For Vancouver, Victoria, and BC municipalities adopting SherpaCarta as a civic digital rights standard.

What cities adopt

  • Zero non-essential tracking on city websites and apps
  • Data sovereignty for resident services (Canadian hosting preference)
  • Algorithmic transparency for automated permit/benefit decisions
  • Annual public digital rights report

Rollout steps

  1. Council resolution referencing SherpaCarta Arts. 11–13
  2. IT procurement clause update (see ⌘K → Procurement Clause)
  3. Staff training on privacy-by-design
  4. Public endorsement badge on city site
